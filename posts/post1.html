Quotes from "The Art Of Laziness"

January 27, 2026

One day you will wake up, and there won't be any more time to do the things you're always wanted. Do it now

If you want to find a solution, you will find it; it's not that a solution doesn't exist. It exists, and you know it deep inside of your heart.

Stop being dependent on other people.

If you're unhappy, it's your fault.

Even if you fail, that will still be better than the regret of never trying go to push your boundaries.

All of humanity's problems stem from our inability to sit quietly in a room alone.

It may seem difficult at first, but everything is difficult at first.

If you allow yourself 2 hours to finish a task, it will take 2 hours, but if you only have 1 hour, you will find a way to complete the task in an hour.

Time is running out. Start living!
